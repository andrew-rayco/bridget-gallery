The Farm

This is a collection of paintings, watercolours, prints, photographs and sculptures celebrating life on the farm, here and overseas.

Featured is William Ford’s fine chromo-lithograph “The Last of the Drove” 1878 published by the Art Union of Victoria, 1880.
