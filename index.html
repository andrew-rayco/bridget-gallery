Founded in 1983 above an antique shop in High St, Armadale, the Bridget McDonnell Gallery moved to its current loction in Carlton in 1986.
We believe paintings and drawings by major artists are affordable and fine examples can be acquired for much less than often thought.
The Bridget McDonnell Gallery holds regular catalogue exhibitions of Early Australian Watercolours and Drawings, Early Australian Painters, Modern Australian Paintings, Artists Exhibiting in London 1880-1940, Australian Women Artists, Australian Prints and a Christmas exhibition where everything is priced under $1,500. One-man exhibitions have included Sidney Nolan, Mary Hammond, Vic O’Connor, Jan Senbergs, Barry Dickins, Celia Perceval, Brigid Cole Adams, Jeff Ferris and Clifford Bayliss and works by these artists are always in stock.
Gallery services include valuations, re-hanging your paintings, advice regarding sale and purchase of paintings, restoration and framing services.
Visit the gallery to find out more or ask for suggestions.
